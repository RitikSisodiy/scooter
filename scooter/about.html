What We Do

We are engaged in the manufacturing of Electric Vehicles . EVEX is produced by using cutting edge technology; quality approved component and innovative ideas from our in-house RND. Further, we keep the diverse specifications, demands and details given by the clients in mind while developing the whole product range. EVEX is highly recommended for its user friendly nature, long battery backup, advance technology, low maintenance requirement, long functional life, smooth functioning, elegant look, perfect design, accurate dimension and many other features. We offer innovative ideas of our professionals. We are offering vehicle and battery warranty for our Vehicles. Most of the Vehicles components are made from in house and we are on the way to 100 % indigenous product.
